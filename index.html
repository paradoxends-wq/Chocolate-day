Dark Chocolate

Not meant to be rushed.
FIRST BITE
It doesn’t try to be sweet.
It waits.

And when it finally melts,
it asks you to stay.
THE DEPTH
There’s intensity beneath the surface.
Not loud.
Controlled.

The kind you feel slowly,
long after the moment passes.
THE FINISH
The best kind of indulgence
doesn’t disappear.

It lingers.
Quietly.
Exactly where you felt it first.
If you’re holding dark chocolate tonight,
don’t break it yet.
Let it warm for a second.
