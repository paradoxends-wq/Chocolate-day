Lindt.

Take it slowly.
Before the first bite,
there’s a pause.
The quiet crackle. The moment of intent.
The first bite isn’t sweet.
It’s composed.
It doesn’t ask to be liked.
The second bite stays longer.
Slower.
This is where preference becomes choice.
Some things don’t need sweetness.
They need intention.
I know what this means to you.
And you deserve beautiful things in this world.

Just a small token of attention from my side — given with intention.
— N
