Dark Chocolate

Not meant to be rushed.
85% CACAO
Dark chocolate doesn’t announce itself.
It waits.

The first moment is intense.
Almost challenging.

And then — it settles.
I’ve noticed you’re the same.

Not immediately sweet.
Not trying to be easy.

But the kind of presence
you keep thinking about
once you’ve had a taste.
Some flavors don’t fade when the moment ends.

They linger.
Quietly.

On purpose.
If you have dark chocolate with you tonight,
let it melt slowly.

The best parts always do.
